📝 1-Paragraph Pitch Summary

Zundamon's Kitchen V2 is a heartwarming Roblox culinary life-simulator where players harvest wild ingredients across Zunda Village, cook rhythm-game dishes to upbeat anime tracks, decorate their dream restaurant plot, and team up with adorable pea-spirit companions. Powered by a high-performance Matter ECS engine, it brings high-craft cozy gameplay to players of all ages worldwide.
